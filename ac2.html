Noun

Theme - So today, we learnt about what nouns are and their different types. Nouns are words that name people, places, things, or ideas. They help us identify and refer to specific entities in our language.

    The different types of nouns are -

  • Proper noun
  • Common noun
  • Collective noun
  • Abstract noun
  • Concrete noun
